About Us

We are a maritime agency based in Nigeria that provide numerous nautical and shipping services to clients around the Gulf of Guinea. Our clients across numerous industries trust our service delivery and our commitment to excellence. We value our clients that's why we put your needs first and ensure that we deliver comprehensive and world-class value. Experience the oceans in a new way.

What we do

SYLVANGEL MARITIME RESOURCES LIMITED offers top-notch maritime services based on international Standards. Our expertise lies in Ship Agency, Crewing/Manning Services, Chartering & Ship Brokerage, Marine Equipment Hiring, Leasing & purchasing, Marine Consulting/Superintendent Service and Procurement Services catered towards the Cil and Gas industry.

SERVICES

AT SYLVANGEL MARITIME RESOURCES, we provide the highest quality services coupled with a seamless customer experience to ensure all your needs are met. we are in a mission to redefine service delivery in the maritime industry and our capacity is tested in that regard.
These are some of the services we proffer.
SHIPPING Agency & MGT.

Our management team efficiently overseas vessels for our client at our seaports, ensuring that they are maintained and operated with the utmost diligence and dedication to achieve optimal efficiency. Your vessels are under the care of world-class professional when you solicit our management services.

CHARTERING & BROKERAGE

Our connections with numerous ship owners and charterers allows us to offer you the best prices for a wide selection of marine vessels. Our chartering services extend to a diverse range of vessels including bulk tanker, liner, tramp, supply, seismic, and survey vessels. We also manage port operation, provide marine equipment rental, leasing, and sales, as well as handle export operations.

MANNING & CREWING

We provide our client with efficient and professional crew members who have been registered with NIMASA.
